Gợi ý nhanh cho Extractor (mở ra xem)
  • Regex (HTML): name="_csrf" value="(.+?)", window.__SESSION_ID__\s*=\s*"(.+?)", href="/product/view\?id=(\d+)" (Match No = -1).
  • JSONPath: $.data.access_token, $.products[*].name, $.items[*].sku, $.order.order_id.
  • XPath 2.0 (XML): //sec:token/@value, //auth/token/text(), comment token: Regex SECRET_TOKEN=([A-Z0-9\-]+).
  • Decode: Base64 trong comment → JSR223(Groovy) Base64; GZIP+Base64 → GZIPInputStream.
